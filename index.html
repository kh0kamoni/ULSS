Installation Guide

  • 1
    Download & Unpack
    Click the download button above to download the latest universal-live-stream-sync.zip file. Once downloaded, extract/unzip the contents to a folder on your computer.
  • 2
    Open Extension Settings
    Open your Chromium-based browser (Chrome, Edge, Brave, Opera) and navigate to the extensions management page:
    chrome://extensions (or edge://extensions in Microsoft Edge).
  • 3
    Enable Developer Mode
    Locate the Developer mode toggle switch (usually found in the top-right corner of the extensions page) and turn it ON.
  • 4
    Load the Extension
    Click the Load unpacked button (located in the top-left corner of the page). In the file browser dialog that appears, select the folder where you extracted the ZIP archive in Step 1. (Select the folder containing the manifest.json file).
